Frequently Asked Questions in Nappanee, IN

What size roll off dumpster rental do I actually need?

The best way to know is to call us and describe your project. But as a general rule, a 10 yard dumpster is for small cleanouts and remodels, a 20 yard dumpster is for larger renovations and cleanouts, and a 30 yard dumpster is for major construction or whole-home purges.

What can I put in my roll off rental?

You can put most common household and construction debris in our dumpsters. This includes furniture, wood, drywall, roofing shingles, yard waste, and appliances.

Is there anything I can't put in the dumpster?

Yes, hazardous materials are not allowed. This includes paints, chemicals, oils, asbestos, and flammable liquids. We can provide you with a full list of prohibited items

Nappanee, IN Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Nappanee is a city in Elkhart and Kosciusko counties in the U.S. state of Indiana. The population was 6,648 as of the 2010 U.S. Census and had grown to 6,913 by the 2020 U.S. Census. The name Nappanee probably means "flour" in Algonquian. The town has several tourist attractions: Amish Acres, Nappanee Raceway, The Arts & Crafts Festival, and the Apple Festival.
